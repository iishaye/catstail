    • ABOUT Cat's Tail

    Welcome to Cat's Tail, where cozy vibes meet feline charm!

    Our café is more than just a place to enjoy your favorite coffee or tea— it's a sanctuary for cat lovers, offering a purrfectly relaxing environment to unwind with playful and friendly cats. Whether you're looking for a peaceful moment to read a book, catch up with friends, or simply enjoy the company of adorable, adoptable cats, Cat's Tail provides the ideal setting.

    We believe in the power of cats to bring joy, comfort, and connection. Our resident cats are lovingly cared for and are always eager to interact with visitors in a safe and calm environment. You can pet, play, and even take selfies with them while sipping on handcrafted beverages and indulging in delicious pastries.

    At Cat's Tail, we’re passionate about promoting animal welfare. Many of our cats are available for adoption, so you might even meet your future feline friend. Come visit us for a truly unique experience that celebrates both the love of coffee and cats.

    Join us at Cat's Tail and make some memories that will leave you smiling long after you’ve left!
